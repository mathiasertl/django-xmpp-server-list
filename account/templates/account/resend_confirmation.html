Confirming your contact details

Both your email-address and your JID have to be verified if you want to publish servers on this site. To this end, you will receive automated messages containing a validation URL on the respective account. You just have to open the validation URL to confirm your address.

    {% if not user.profile.email_confirmed %}
  • An email containing a verification URL has been sent to {{ user.email }}.
    • {% endif %} {% if not user.profile.jid_confirmed %}

  • An XMPP message containing a verification URL has been sent to {{ user.profile.jid }}.

    Note: The verification message will come from {{ jid }}. Some clients block messages from accounts not in your buddy-list, so you might have to explicitely allow messages from that account.

    • {% endif %}

If you haven't received a confirmation message, you can just reload this page.
